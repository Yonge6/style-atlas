虾子曰艺术风格图鉴隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-07-04

中文

虾子曰艺术风格图鉴是一款视觉风格图鉴和审美学习 App。首版不需要登录，不收集姓名、手机号、邮箱等个人身份信息。

你的收藏数据保存在本地设备中。App 不上传用户内容，不包含用户生成内容功能，不出售用户数据，也不接入第三方广告。

首版不启用真实 App 内购买。如果未来启用 Plus，购买和支付记录将由 Apple In-App Purchase 处理，App 只读取购买状态以解锁对应功能。

如果未来加入统计或分析功能，我们会在启用前更新本隐私政策，说明使用的工具、收集的数据类型和用途。

English

Xiazishuo Style Atlas is a visual style atlas and aesthetic learning app. The first version does not require login and does not collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal identity information.

Saved styles are stored locally on your device. The app does not upload user content, does not include user-generated content features, does not sell user data, and does not use third-party advertising.

The first version does not enable real in-app purchases. If Plus is enabled in the future, purchases and payment records will be handled by Apple In-App Purchase. The app will only read purchase status to unlock eligible features.

If analytics or tracking features are added in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before they are enabled, including the tools used, data types collected, and purposes.
