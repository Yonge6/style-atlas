# App Review Notes｜虾子曰艺术风格图鉴 1.2

## English

Xiazishuo Style Atlas is an offline visual style atlas and aesthetic learning app. Version 1.2 improves stability, image-loading performance, export reliability, and accessibility. No account or login is required, and the 120 core style covers and records are bundled with the app for offline use.

The app offers one existing non-consumable in-app purchase:

- Product ID: `xiazishuo_style_atlas_plus_lifetime`
- Product: Xiazishuo Style Atlas Plus lifetime access

To review the purchase flow:

1. Open the menu from the top-right button.
2. Select Style Atlas Plus.
3. Wait for the localized product and Apple-provided price to load.
4. Tap Unlock Plus.

Restore Purchases is available on the same Plus screen. Plus unlocks all 120 complete style archives, unlimited saved styles, complete style expressions, and high-resolution watermark-free exports in multiple aspect ratios.

The app does not use external payment. Purchase and restoration are handled exclusively by Apple StoreKit.

虾子曰艺术风格图鉴是一款支持离线使用的视觉风格图鉴与审美学习 App。1.2 版本改善了稳定性、图片加载性能、导出可靠性和无障碍体验。App 不需要账号或登录，120 种风格的核心封面与档案均随 App 本地打包，可离线浏览。

App 提供一个已存在的非消耗型 App 内购买项目：

- Product ID：`xiazishuo_style_atlas_plus_lifetime`
- 商品：虾子曰艺术风格图鉴 Plus 终身解锁

审核购买流程：

1. 点击右上角菜单按钮。
2. 选择 Style Atlas Plus。
3. 等待商品名称和 Apple 返回的本地价格加载。
4. 点击“解锁 Plus”。

“恢复购买”位于同一 Plus 页面。Plus 解锁全部 120 个完整风格档案、无限收藏、完整风格表达词，以及多比例高清无水印导出。

App 不提供外部支付，购买与恢复购买仅通过 Apple StoreKit 完成。
